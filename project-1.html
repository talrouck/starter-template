Mutiny House Theatre

MUTINY HOUSE THEATRE is a fictional, Vancouver based theatre created with the intention of making underground, abstract, and queer theatre more accessible and approachable to a mainstream audience. Utilizing a modern visual language inspired by classic sailing imagery, symbols, and culture, Mutiny takes patrons on a journey that leaves them changed for the better (no sea-faring experience required).
